    About Me

    Christopher Adkinson, also known by his pen name, Merry Tragic, is a freelance illustrator and long-time superfan of comic books. He specializes in digital illustration and color work and especially enjoys drawing animals and people.

    Christopher lives and works in Seattle Washington, with his wife and small herd of cats. He currently has 2,309 comic books in his collection.
