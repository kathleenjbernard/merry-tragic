    About

    Merry Tragic is an art brand owned and operated by Christopher Atkinson, freelance illustrator and long-time comic book superfan. Having filled a seemingly endless number of journals growing up, Chris fell in love with digital art in high school and things haven't been the same since!

    Christopher resides near Seattle Washington with his wife and four cats, where he continues to grow Merry Tragic.
